Welcome to Duluth!

Discover the beauty of my college town, where Lake Superior meets the hills and creates a stunning backdrop for education and adventure. As a student at the University of Minnesota Duluth, I've found a perfect blend of natural beauty, academic excellence, and vibrant community life.

Why I Picked UMD
Before visiting UMD, I never imagined myself going here. But as soon as I toured the campus, everything changed — it just felt like home. The stunning scenery and natural beauty made a lasting impression on me, and I'm sure many others have felt that same sense of belonging.
Why I Love Duluth
Lake Superior at Lester Park
Before visiting Duluth, I didn't realize just how amazing this city really is. The true highlight is Lake Superior, which makes me feel at home and is my favorite part of the city!
  • Endless hiking trails perfect for exploring and taking in the scenery
  • A great balance between lively city life and peaceful nature escapes
  • Canal Park never disappoints, and watching massive ships pass under the Lift Bridge is always a sight to see
  • Each season brings something unique, even winter has its charm
  • The food scene is incredible, with a wide variety of restaurants that make it easy to find something new and delicious every time
Student Life at UMD
Some of my favorite parts of being a student here include...
  • The campus size is perfect, not too big, not too small
  • Cooking and eating dinner with my friends, just being able to talk and lose track of time
  • Those perfect fall days when you can walk to class in the fresh air
  • Getting hyped at hockey games and campus events
  • The library is such a cozy space to study and a great place to access professional help with you homework
  • Weekend hikes or spontaneous trips up the North Shore to explore new places and views
  • Going downtown on the weekends is always a fun time with my friends
  • Watching the sunset over Lake Superior after a long day and realizing how lucky you are to be here

Spots You Can't Miss
Chester Park
These are the places my friends and I always end up at:
  • Park Point is my friends and I's favorite spot to visit year round
  • Enger Tower trust, the climb is worth it for the view!
  • The Lakewalk is great for walks, runs, or just chilling by the water
  • Chester Park trails when you need a quick escape into nature
  • Lester Park at sunset is my favorite spot to catch the most amazing golden hour views
Best Eats in Duluth
My go-to food spots in Duluth
  • Sir Benedicts - amazing sandwiches and appetizers with a great view of Lake Superior
  • Portland Malt Shoppe - seasonal ice cream spot that's totally worth the wait, they have so many flavors to choose from
  • Harbor 360 - when you want that fancy dinner with an INCREDIBLE view of the harbor
  • Cloud 9 - All you can eat menu is a go-to, my favorite sushi spot and in Canal!
  • Tavern on the Hill - okay, I'll be honest, I literally go here just for the cheese curds... they're so good!
